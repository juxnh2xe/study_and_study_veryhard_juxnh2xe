# DESIGN.md - Early Morning Sky (새벽하늘) Design System Specification (v2.0)

---

## 1. Design Philosophy & Dynamic Sky Theme

The **Early Morning Sky** design system combines the clarity of **Apple Human Interface Guidelines (HIG)** with the crisp precision of **Linear Design**.

v2.0 introduces **Dynamic Sky Theme** where the background ambient glow subtly shifts according to the local hour:
- **Dawn (새벽 04~07시)**: `#070A0F` (Midnight Deep)
- **Morning (아침 07~12시)**: `#0A101D` (Morning Glow)
- **Day (낮 12~18시)**: `#0B1222` (Day Sky Deep)
- **Sunset (노을 18~21시)**: `#100F1C` (Sunset Violet Shadow)
- **Night (밤 21~04시)**: `#070A0F` (Midnight Deep)

Deep Black + Sky Blue core accent identity is preserved throughout all phases.

## 2. v2.0 Premium Experience Components

- **SplashScreen**: Brand logo load overlay with gentle 350ms fade transition.
- **OnboardingModal**: Carousel guide modal for new users with step indicators.
- **FocusGarden**: Level-based growth card (Cloud -> Sprout -> Tree -> Forest -> Starry Night).
- **AnalyticsReportModal**: Detailed Weekly & Monthly report cards with AI insight highlights.
